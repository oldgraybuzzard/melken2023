Interactive Electronic Technical Manuals (IETM)

Upgrade your technical game with IETMs - interactive, intuitive, and instant access to all your technical documentation needs.

What is an IETM?

It is a digital manual or technical documentation used by various industries, such as aviation, military, and manufacturing, to provide users with interactive access to technical information. An IETM typically text, graphics, videos, animations, and other multimedia elements that allow users to navigate and understand complex technical information easily. It is designed to be accessed on electronic devices such as computers, tablets, and smartphones, and can be updated and distributed more efficiently than traditional paper-based technical manuals. IETMs have become increasingly popular due to their ease of use, accessibility, and the ability to provide real-time information to users.
